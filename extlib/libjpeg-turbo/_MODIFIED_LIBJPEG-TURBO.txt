This copy of libjpeg-turbo-1.5.2 is a modified version of the original.

The following changes have been made to the original:

- autoconf build infratsructure has been removed.

- Files not necessary for building libjpeg-turbo have been removed.

- java/ and md5/ have been removed.

- CMakeLists.txt has been modified to disable building of test and
  example programs.

To obtain the original libjpeg-turbo-1.5.2, visit:
http://libjpeg-turbo.virtualgl.org/
